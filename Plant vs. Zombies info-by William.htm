The game seems to be easy at the begginning, but new players, trust me, it will be fun when after the boring part. The zombies will gets harder to defeat after the each round.
And there are different kinds of levels, like the normal levels (you collect the sun and plant), the conveyer belt levels (which you get plants from it), the special levels (which is made up from the spcial zombies in the current wourld), and the the boss level (which you have to defeat the zomboss).
